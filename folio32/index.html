About Peacocks

Peacocks are basically male peafowls, while peahens are women peafowls. Peacocks are omnivores (both a carnivore and herbivore) they eat both plants and other animals (like insects). Peacocks are mostly known for their beautiful brightly colored feathers that alsmost look like eyes (which is kind of creepy but still cool). Peacocks actually have tiny crystal-like structures between each of their feathers. These crystal-like stuctures bend and reflect light in attractive ways, which makes the bird more striking and vibrant. All peacocks can actually fly, which amazed me at first since there so big. They are actually one of the most biggest birds that can fly, reaching up to 5 feet in length (including their tail). The Tale of the Peacock Rain Dance

The tale of the peacock rain dance is basically a tale that people believed for centuries and still do. Its about how peacocks tend to dance (shake their feathers) before rain comes. They belived that they can fortell when rain would come. This though is not scientifically proven, people have experienced them dancing before rain came. But scientist believe its just a coincidence and that the peacocks were just at the right place, at the right time (which kind of sucks because I was hoping it was real).
