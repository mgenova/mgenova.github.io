French Toast

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Description

This is french toast, it is my most favorite breakfast food. It tastes delicious, it's quick and easy to make, and it's all in all a perfect and a good breakfast food.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • cinnamon
  • butter
  • one loaf of bread
  • mixing bowl or big normal bowl
  • a fork or whisk
  • a spatula
  • a pan (you also need a stove for this but you probably already knew that)
  • powdered sugar (optional)
  • syrup (optional)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Directions

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Then your gonna want to bring out your mixing bowl
  3. Next, your gonna crack open your two eggs and put it into the mixing bowl and then whisk that with a fork or whisk until fully mixed
  4. Then your gonna add in the 1/4 cup of milk and the teaspoon of vanilla extract, in with the eggs in the bowl and mix them all together
  5. Then sprinkle some cinnamon into the egg mixure and stir (this can vary depending on how much cinnamon you want for the taste)
  6. After that, heat up your pan and when hot enough put butter into the pan
  7. Then, get your bread loaf and take two breads, dip one into the mixure and put it on the pan and repeat this again for the next bread
  8. Next, get your cinnamon and sprinkle a little bit of cinnamon on the top of each bread thats on the pan
  9. When one side is done cooking, your gonna flip it over and wait for the other side to cook.
  10. When finished, your gonna put the french toast on a plate and your gonna butter it (if you want), and then sprinkle powdered sugar or add syrup (optional)
  11. Then serve. The mixure is enough to feed two or three people but if your feeding more, you can make more mixure along the way or add bigger servings
  12. Enjoy!
