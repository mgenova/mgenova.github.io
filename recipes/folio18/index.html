The Greatest Banana Cream Pie

Description

This kind of Banana Cream Pie is the best Pie in the world. It has 1 layer of each the crust, cream cheese, banana's, Banana puding, and finaly whip cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 3/4 cup flour
  • 1 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 5 bananas
  • 8 oz whipping cream
  • 2 boxes vanilla pudding
  • 3 cup milk
  • 1 table spoon white sugar
  • 1 tee spoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Bake crust
  2. By hand mix, 1 cup Butter, and 1 3/4 cup flour, with 1/4 cup powdered sugar.
  3. Bake @ 356 fahrenheit for 25 minutes.
  4. Mix 8 oz cream cheese with 8 oz whipping cream, and 1 cup powdered sugar.
  5. Spread evenly on top of crust.
  6. Slice bananas into quarter shaped pieces lay evenly ontop layer #1.
  7. Mix both 4 oz vanilla puding packages with 3 cup milk speard pudding out evenly over the bananas.
  8. Mix 8 oz whipping cream with 1 tps white sugar and 1 tsp vanilla extract.
  9. spread final layer evenly on Banana pudding.
  10. Serve
