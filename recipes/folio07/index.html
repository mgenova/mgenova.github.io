Chocolate Covered Strawberries

Description

This chocolate covered fruit is just right for me because it is both healthy and sweet.

Ingredients

  • Small bowl
  • Chocolate chips
  • Microwave
  • Strawberries
  • Parchment paper
  • Baking sheet
  • Crisco
  • Spoon(not required)
  • Freezer

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients and put them on a clear counter.
  2. Pour chocolate chips and crisco into the small bowl.
  3. Put the bowl of chocolate chips in the microwave and micowave for 30 seconds then stir.
  4. Repeat step 3 until chocolate chips are melted.
  5. Grab your parchment paper and cover your baking sheet with it.
  6. Dip your strawberries into the bowl of melted chocolate, if you have a spoon you can use it to get the chocolate on the top of the strawberry.
  7. Quickly lay your strawberries on the parchment paper so the chocolate doesn't melt and fall right off the srawberry.
  8. stick the baking sheet in the freezer and wait till the chocolate is frozen.
  9. Now you can eat your delicious chocolate covered strawberry
  10. You can also put other think on your strawberry like crushed nuts by sprinkling them on the strawberry right before you put it in the freezer.
