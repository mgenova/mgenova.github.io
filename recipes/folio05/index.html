The Perfect Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Macaroni and cheese have a perfect mix of savory flavors, coming from the noodles, and the cheese.

Ingredients

  • One box of elbow noodles
  • A pack of any kind of cheese
  • A container to bake with
  • A pot

Directions

  1. Boil a pot of water
  2. Add elbow noodles to water to cook
  3. When the elbow noodles are done cooking, add them to the baking container.
  4. Add your prefered cheese on top of the elbow noodles
  5. Bake
  6. Bam! You're done, and can enjoy your food.
