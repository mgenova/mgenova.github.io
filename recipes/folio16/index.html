TUNARIFFIC

Description

This is the best tuna around. NOTHING IS GOING TO BRING IT DOWN. That aside this is my favorite thing to make and give to my family.

Tools required

  • Can opener
  • Bowl
  • Spoon

Ingrediants

  • Canned tuna of your choice
  • Olives optinal
  • salt or garlic salt
  • pepper
  • Mayonnaise
  • Relish dill or sweet
  1. First use the can opener to open th tuna. Be carful here and do it over a sink. You'll want to open it to where there is still a bit of metal connected. Push down gently and get all liquid out.
  2. Next You'll want to do the same with the olives is their canned. Just don't push down just empty the liquid.
  3. After that add both ingredients to the bowl you chose. You'll want to make sure it's big to fit everything.
  4. Next add the salt, peper, and mayo.
  5. Start mixing and adding spices and mayo if you think it needs more.
When you're done making your tuna you can eat it by itself or on other things. For example you can put it on bread or with crackers.
