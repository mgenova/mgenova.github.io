The Perfect Caramel Popcorn

Description

Have you ever found plain old popcorn a little bland? Here's a little recipe to sweeten things up! This caramel popcorn is just the thing to make a movie at home more enjoyable than any theater experience!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 10 large marshmellows
  • 14oz air popped popcorn
  • Saucepan
  • Mixing bowl
  • Serving bowl
  • wooden cooking spoon
  • Stove

Directions

  1. Take the saucepan and combine the sugar, butter, and marshmellows in the pan, and put over heat.
  2. Mix the ingredients over low heat with wooden spoon until melted and combined into caramel.
  3. Take the mixing bowl and dump the popcorn into it.
  4. While still hot, pour caramel over the popcorn.
  5. Mix the popcorn and hot caramel in the bowl with the wooden spoon.
  6. When each piece of popcorn is covered in caramel, and the caramel is no longer hot, pour the contents of the mixing bowl into the serving bowl.

Now you have your caramel popcorn! Sit down with friends (or not) and enjoy a well earned snack to satisfy your sweet tooth!
