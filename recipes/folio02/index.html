coconut macaroon recipe

Description

Craving coconut? whip up these delicious coconut macaroons in no time for a sweet treat! They are crisp on the outside and soft and sweet on the inside. Not only are these cookies super yummy, they are also only 5 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 cups shredded coconut
  • 2/3 cups sweetend condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • chocolate syrup

Directions

  1. Preheat oven ti 325*F (170*C)
  2. Stir the flour and coconut together in a large mixing bowl
  3. Add the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla and mix well
  4. Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper or grease it
  5. drop the dough by tablespoons onto the cookie sheet. Make sure the cookies are at least 2 inches apart
  6. Bake in oven for 15 minuets, or until light brown.
  7. Remove from oven
  8. Immediatley remove the cookies from the cookie sheet with a spatula onto a cooling rack
  9. let cool for 10 minuets
  10. Drizzle with chocolate
  11. Serve
