The perfect Hamburger

Description

This hamburger is the most perfect burger ever. It has the best taste. Every bite is so juicy and tasty.

Ingredients

    1. 2 slices of hamburger bread
    2. 1 hamburger patty
    3. lettuce
    4. Tomato
    5. mayonase
    6. Pickles
    7. A grill
    8. Cheese

Directions

    1. Lay the patties on the grill
    2. Take the patties off the grill afetr about 10min
    3. Put the mayonase on the bottom part of the bread.
    4. Put the patty on the bottom part of the bread
    5. Put 1 slice of lettuce on top of the patty
    6. Put the cheese on top of the lettuce
    7. Put 3 slices of pickles on top of the tomato
    8. Put the top of the bread on top of the pickles
    9. Put 3 slices of tomato on top of the lettuce
    10. Enjoy the hamburger!!!!!!!!!
