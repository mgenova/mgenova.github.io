Basil Avocado Pasta

Description

This is a quick and healthy pasta I like to make! It's super delicious and takes roughly 15 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients

  • any kind of pasta noodles
  • 1/4 a cup of mint
  • half an avocado
  • water
  • 1/4 a lemon
  • salt
  • 2 garlic cloves

Directions

  1. Put some water into a pot and place the pot on a stove. (high heat)
  2. Wait until water comes to a boil. When it starts to boil, put the noodles in.
  3. While the noodles are cooking, wash and cut the avocado, lemon, mint, and garlic.
  4. Put the lemon, avocado, garlic, and mint into a blender or food precessor. .
  5. When the mixture is smooth and creamy, add salt to taste.
  6. Strain the noodles and then pour the sauce on top.
  7. Enjoy your meal!
