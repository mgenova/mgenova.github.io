Lasagna!!!!!

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish that is basically a caserole made of long, flat noodles, tomato sauce, meat, and cheese! This is a dish best served hot, not warm or cold. It is not too salty, and is yummier than spaghetti. So let's eat some lasagna! Oh wait, we don't have any...not a problem! I'm going going to show you how to make lasagna!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Montery Jack Cheese :)
  • Ground beef (can be replaced with spinach for vegetarians)
  • Lasgna tray
  • Butter
  • 4 thsp of olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • minced onion

    • Directions To Awesomness (The Lasagna)

    1. Boil the noodles.
    2. Grate cheese
        Do this while you are boiling the noodles
    3. Cook the ground beef
        put in the garlic, olive oil, and minced onions for flavor.
    4. Butter the lasagna pan (so the lasagna won't stick.)
    5. Once the noodles are done boiling, lay them down in the buttered pan.
    6. Spread the tomato sauce on top of the noodles.
    7. sprinkle da cheese on the noodles. Not just a tiny sprinkle, though, put on LOT'S of cheese, please!
    8. Pack on the meat.
    9. Do steps 5-8 all over again until the desired amount is reached.
    10. Put the lasagna in the oven for 15 minutes at 350 degrees Farenheight --Since the noodles and meat are cooked, we just need to melt the cheese.
    11. Wait.
    12. Take the lasagna out of the oven and let it cool...But not for TOO long.
    13. At last! You can eat the lasagna! YAAAAAAAAAAA!!!!!!!!!!
