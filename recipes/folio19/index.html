Chocolate Peanut Butter Rice Krispies

Description

This is a delicious treat great for any party. It has the delicious peanut butter and chocolate taste with a nice soft crunch. This is definantly one of my favorite treats.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Creamy Peanut Butter

  • 2 cups Semisweet Chocolate Chips

  • 1 cup (2 Sticks) Butter

  • 2 10 1/2 oz bags Mini Marshmallow

  • 8 cups Rice Krispies Cereal

  • Large Saucepan

  • 9x13 pan

  • Non-stick Spray

  • Large Mixing Bowl

Directions

  1. Spray a 9 x 13 pan with non-stick spray and set aside.

  2. Put the peanut butter, chocolate chips, and butter into a large saucepan over medium heat. Stir frequently until fully melted and mixed.

  3. Add marshmallows and stir in until fully melted.

  4. Pour Rice Krispies into large mixing bowl. Add peanut butter and chocolate mix into cereal. Mix until combined.

  5. Spread mixture into prepared pan and smooth out.

  6. Refrigerate for 2-3 hours or until firm.

  7. Use sharp knife to cut into squares

  8. Enjoy Delicious Treat!
