French Toast

Description

French toast is definietly on the top of my breakfast list. It has a sweet, cinnamon taste and can be served with maple syrup. This quick and easy recipe makes 4 servings, and will for sure make people wanting to come back for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 slices of your choice of bread (thick bread is preferred)
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup (optional)
  • powder sugar (optional)

Directions

  1. Stir together cinnamon, nutmeg, sugar, vanilla extract, milk, and eggs together in a small bowl
  2. Melt butter in a large pan over medium heat
  3. Dip bread in egg mixture and place in pan
  4. Cook slices until golden brown, then filp to cook the other side
  5. Place slices on plates and pour maple syrup and sift power sugar over the top if you would like
  6. Serve and enjoy
