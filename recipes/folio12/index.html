Get All The Listed Ingredients~!:

~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~

    • Chicken - Frozen, Fresh, Rotisserie
    • Water - 1/2 Pot of Water
    • Hawaiian Salt - 1-2 tsp
    • Bay Leaves
    • Basil
    • Oregano
    • Carrots - Diced
    • Can of Corn - With Liquid in it - DO NOT DRAIN
    • Agave
    • Garlic - 2 Heaping tbsp
    • Onion - Minced/Diced
    • Pepper
    • Italian Seasonings
    • Potatoes - Chopped
    • Celery - Diced
    • Chicken Broth - 1 Can

~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~

Intructions:

  1. Fil pot 1/2 way with tap water then add chicken (can be frozen), onions and garlic and turn on 'medium high heat.'

    2. ~❀~

  2. Wash and peel the potatoes.

    3. ~❀~

  3. Cut the potatoes into smaller chunks, then add to pot.

    4. ~❀~

  4. Wash and peel carrots and celery.

    5. ~❀~

  5. Dice the celery and chop up the carrots, then add them to the pot.

    6. ~❀~

  6. Add the can of chicken broth and the can of corn (pour in the liquid too).

    7. ~❀~

  7. Add salt, pepper basil, oregano, bay leaves, and Italian seasoning.

    8. ~❀~

  8. Add a squeeze of agave into the pot.

    9. ~❀~

  9. Bring to a boil then turn down heat, let it simmer on the lowest heat setting for about 20 - 30 mins (good tip to check if it's ready is, every now and then poke the carrots and potatoes with a wooden spoon, if it's soft, that means it's ready).

    10. ~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~❀~

    Tidbit!:

If you use frozen chicken, just add it directly to the pot. Wait until the chicken is thorughouly cooked then use tongs and the kitchen scissors to cut it into smaller pieces. If you use rotiserie chicken, add the liquid at the bottom to the pot for added taste.
