The Great Banana

Not so long ago, in a galaxy not so far away, there lived a minion named...Tim.

Tim LOVED bananas.

But, He always dreamed of having the Great Banana

So, Tim gathered his, well, a thousand or so friends and went in search of this great treasure

They searched long and hard for that Great Banana. EVERYWHERE!!!!

Until...

They find the Great Banana Split

They were so happy they decided to throw a party and eat it!!
