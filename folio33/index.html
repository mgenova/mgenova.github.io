Sonic Boom is a Sonic The Hedeghog franchise. It is the most recent one. Sonic Boom is one of the few Sonic The Hedeghog games that has a TV show to go along with it.

The story follows Sonic, his sidekick, Tails, Amy, Knuckles, and the newly introduced Sticks The Badger as they fight to keep their island home, Bygone Island, safe from villians such as the evil Dr. Eggman. This series is full of action as well as comedy.

Sonic The Hedgehog:

Sonic is a blue hedgehog that has the ability to run faster than the speed of sound. His favorite food is chilidogs (no, seriously, he is crazy about those things). He is cocky and arrogant, yet he is a true hero, and cares about his friends.

Miles "Tails" Prower:

Tails is a yellow fox with two tails (hence his nickname). He is Sonic's sidekick and best friend. His unique abnormality allows him to fly. His tails acting as helicopter propellors. Despite the fact that he can fly with his tails, he also flies a biplane.

Amy Rose:

Amy is a pink female hedgehog who has romantic feelings towards Sonic. Although she isn't as desperate or dependant as she was in the older generations of the Sonic franchise, she still has a feirce attitude and a giant hammer. She uses this Thor style hammer to smash robots and other stuff.

Knuckles The Echidna:

Knuckles is a red echidna with dreadlock-ish hair/quills. He is unhumanly strong and has spikes on his knuckles. In this new generation of the Sonic franchise, Knuckles is a dim-witted character, with many funny quotes (that he unwittingly says). In the older generations however, Knuckles was grumpy and was a little too trusting.

Sticks The Badger:

This new addition to the gang is extremly paranoid, is almost always screaming or yelling, and lives in the jungle...by herself. She is loyal to her friends, however, and sometimes, her predictions/worries come true.

Dr. Eggman:

Dr Eggman is the main antagonist of the series. In the older generations, he is bit scarier, and more serious. Now, he is kind of a warm, fuzzy character who plays a board game called Fuzzy Puppy Buddies, and sometimes helps Sonic and the gang with their problems.Eggman has two robot servants named Orbot and Cubot. Eggman is really mean to them.

Changes:

There are quite a few noticable changes for the Sonic Boom series. For Sonic, he has blue arms (In the old generations, he had peach arms) and his spikes are strategically messier. Sonic also wears a brown bandana and tons of sports tape. The spike change, the bandana, the sports tape, and the blue arms are probably the most noticeable characteristics that have been changed or added to our favorite blue hedgehog.

Our flying fox, Tails has gotten some changes as well. He now wears sports tape on his shoes, and wears steampunk goggles and welding gloves. He does more inventing and is one of the smartest people on Bygone Island.

Our heroine, Amy, has also changed in appearence as well as additude. Her dress isn't as wide and is no longer red. She wears purple sports tape on her arms, and regular sports tape on her waist. She isn't as lovestruck/crazy or dependent on Sonic any more. She is more mature, and is a feminist.

Knuckles probably has the most changes out of all the characters. He is a lot taller and muscular. He wears sports tape (like almost everyone else). Despite all these cool changes, however, there is one thing that isn't as cool (but is amusing): Knuckles has been turned dumb!

Of course, we have to include the bad guy and HIS changes, right? Eggman's appearnce is similar to those of the older generations, but of course, there are changes. He is no longer egg shaped, and wears an out fit that resembles a general's.

Characters:

The main characters have remained...but some of the other's have left the show/series. For example, Sally Acorn, Cream The Rabbit, Antoine and Bunnie D'Coolet, Big The Cat, Blaze The Cat, Silver The Hedgehog, Rouge The Bat, Charmy The Bee, Espio The Chameleon, and many others have been erased from history (just kidding, you can still find them in older generations, but they have been erased from Sonic Boom).

Despite all of these erased characters, however, there are still some old ones that that have brought back...

Shadow The Hedgehog:

This black hedgehog (with some red stripes here and there) is a dark and grumpy character. Unlike his older generations, he is no longer part of Team Dark, and is a bad guy! But don't worry, my fellow Shadow fans, he is still emo and still can teleport (exept he doesn't yell "Chaos Control!" or carry around a Chaos Emerald.)

Shadow has been featured in a Sonic Boom videogame and three Sonic Boom episodes. He hates Sonic and tries to destroy him... One downside about Shadow is that he has REALLY bad reasons for wanting to destroy Sonic. In his first Sonic Boom epsiode he was featured in, he said to Sonic "Your Shoddy craftsmenship brings shame to all hedgehog kind. And for that, you shall perish." (Sonic Boom, Season 1, Episode 51, It Takes A Village To Defeat A Hedgehog). And in his second debute, Eggman tricked Shadow (with a lame video) that Sonic was talking trash about him.

Vector The Crocodile (A.K.A Vector The Detector)

Vector The Crocodile is a classic character. He has been in a few videogames and another Sonic TV show (Sonic X). One big change, however, is that Vector is no longer accompanied by his buddies, Espio and Charmy. Vector also has changed in appearnce: He no longer wears headphones, and wears a vest.

He was first featured in an episode where Amy's hammer had been stolen, and he was sent to find it along with Sonic.

Metal Sonic:

Metal Sonic is Eggman's robotic creation. He resembles Sonic and tries to destroy him... like most of Eggman's robots do.

Perks:

Sonic Boom has many perks. One of them includes Sonamy! For those of you who are new Sonic fans, Sonamy is when there are romantic hints between Sonic and Amy. It is well known in the fandom that Sonic and Amy BOTH have a crush on each other!

Another perk is that Sonic Boom's theme song is super short. This means one doesn't have to listen to "Gotta go fast!" or "Sonic Underground!" For 20 minutes (but some of you may like the theme songs, so I can't judge).

Sonic Boom is also really comical. It makes people (especially me) happy. Unlike some other Sonic shows, where there is TERRIBLE humor and lots of crying.
