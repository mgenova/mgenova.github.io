The Adventure of Snake Boy

On a night in Africa there was a boy playing around and skipping rocks in the savannas where there were lots of rocky hills. That night he found himself confronting a black mamba, these are very rare and unseen, to say the leaset his rock was no match for this poisonous snake. As soon as the fangs dug into his arm his eyes started going green and venom was seeping out of his mouth.

That night Liam was feeling tired and walked home, the next morning Liam woke up to find a pile of snake like skin next to him. Liam walked over to it and as he lifted it up realized it was shaped like him.

That day he realized his life will never be the same he had to go into hiding. All this meant that he would never be able to see his family or friends again, because it would only be putting there lives in danger. So this meant he was now SNAKE BOY the super hero.Now he could team up with all the other superhero's saving the world one power at a time.
